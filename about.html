About Megan Le

I'm a dedicated UX designer with a background in graphic design and photography, blending creative intuition and rational design thinking to craft meaningful user experiences. My time at Bradley University immersed me in the world of design and fine art, cultivating an appreciation for the intersection of artistry and communication. To explore the digital realm further, I studied UX design and frontend development with CareerFoundry in September 2022.

When I'm not immersed in design, I indulge in my passions, such as reading, capturing moments with my camera, and nurturing my indoor plants. Currently, I'm actively seeking a remote, full-time role with a company specializing in financial services or a service-oriented industry. By combining my expertise and passion, I believe we can create impactful and meaningful solutions. Let's connect and bring our creative ideas to life!
