About

My Story

I'm a UX designer dedicated to crafting meaningful experiences that resonate with users. Drawing from my background in graphic design and photography, I bring a unique blend of creative intuition and rational design thinking to my work.

During my years at Bradley University, I immersed myself in the world of design and fine art. This experience cultivated a deep appreciation for the intersection of artistry and communication. Eager to explore the digital realm further, I embarked on a new journey, studying UX design and frontend development with CareerFoundry in September 2022.

When I'm not immersed in design, you'll often find me indulging in my other passions. I enjoy losing myself in the pages of a captivating book, capturing moments through the lens of my camera, and nurturing my indoor plants.

Currently, I'm on the lookout for a remote, full-time role with a company specializing in financial services or a service-oriented industry. I believe that combining my expertise and passion can lead to meaningful and impactful solutions. If you're interested in exploring possibilities or collaborating on exciting projects, let's connect and bring our creative ideas to life!
